Rohit Sharma

Introduction

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the India national cricket team in all formats.
He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional right-arm off break bowler.

Early Life

Rohit was born in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra. He was raised by his grandparents and uncles in Borivali due to his father's limited income.

Career Highlights

  • Made ODI debut in 2007.
  • Known for scoring three double centuries in ODIs.
  • Captain of Mumbai Indians in IPL, winning 5 titles.
  • Appointed India captain in all formats in 2022.
